Kennel Management System

Pawloyalty, the Kennel Management System, is a Software As a Service (SAAS) product built on top of the Microsoft Technology Stack, catering to 350+ pet care providers in the USA and Canada

Live Link

Project Overview

Pawloyalty sets the standard as a premier kennel management system, offering unparalleled efficiency and organization to over 350 pet care service providers across the USA and Canada. Tailored specifically for the dynamic needs of pet care businesses, Pawloyalty streamlines operations with its comprehensive suite of features. From seamlessly managing appointments for daycare, boarding, and grooming services to meticulously tracking pet vaccination records, Pawloyalty ensures that every aspect of pet care administration is effortlessly handled.

With Pawloyalty, pet care providers can effortlessly engage with their clients through personalized SMS reminders for upcoming appointments and strategic email marketing campaigns. The system simplifies the check-in and check-out process for pets, providing a hassle-free experience for both owners and staff. Moreover, Pawloyalty empowers businesses to optimize their workforce by efficiently assigning employees for appointments and facilitating online payments through trusted platforms like Stripe and Card Connect. By offering robust reporting capabilities, Pawloyalty enables businesses to gain valuable insights into their operations, fostering growth and success in the competitive pet care industry.
