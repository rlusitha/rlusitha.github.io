Event Management System

Crafted an event management system for Facets Sri Lanka Gem & Jewellery Exhibition using .NET 8 backend & Angular 16 frontend.

Project Overview

I engineered a dynamic event management system tailored to streamline every facet of event coordination and execution. This robust platform encompasses a spectrum of essential functionalities, from seamless event creation and scheduling to meticulous attendee registration and management. With integrated ticketing and payment processing features, the system effortlessly handles transactions, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both organizers and attendees

Furthermore, the system offers sophisticated venue selection and management tools, empowering organizers to curate unforgettable event experiences. Effective communication and notifications, facilitated through email and SMS, keep stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the event lifecycle. Leveraging cutting-edge QR code technology, the system enables efficient ticket generation and implements QR code scanning capabilities for swift attendee check-in. Additionally, comprehensive feedback and survey collection mechanisms empower organizers to gather valuable insights, facilitating continual improvement and enhancing future event planning endeavors
